## Lane overview

<!-- MANUAL:LANE_OVERVIEW:BEGIN -->
- Boundary (in/out of scope): solid/shell edge fillets and their builder pipeline (contours, stripes, corner filling, result stitching); excludes “input cleanup” (shape healing) except as a prerequisite when fillets fail due to bad input.
- Canonical scenario: fillet a box edge with a small radius (should succeed), then try an oversized radius (should fail with a classified contour status).
- Observable outputs: per-contour `StripeStatus` (`ChFiDS_ErrorStatus`), `IsDone` vs partial result (`HasResult`/`BadShape`), plus result validity + topology counts/bbox as sanity checks.
- Key invariants/tolerances to remember: fillets are local remodeling with continuity constraints; “done” is not the same as “valid”; many failures are contour-local and show up as `StartsolFailure` (often radius/geometry), `WalkingFailure` (propagation), or corner-related issues.
<!-- MANUAL:LANE_OVERVIEW:END -->
